NeedLink
Connecting Donors with NGOs in Need
Technical Overview
What is NeedLink?
NeedLink is a platform that connects donors (people who want to give) with NGOs (organizations that help communities). NGOs post what they need — food, clothing, medicine, school supplies — and donors can find those needs, make a pledge, and track their donation from start to delivery.
The Three Parts of the System
Mobile App
The app donors and NGO staff use every day — browse needs, make pledges, track deliveries, manage requests.
Web Portal
A website for NGOs to register their organization and manage their account before using the mobile app.
Database
Stores all data securely — user accounts, NGO profiles, donation needs, pledges, and delivery records.
Who Uses NeedLink
Donor
A person who wants to donate items to a good cause.
  • Browse donation needs posted by NGOs
  • Filter by category (food, clothing, medicine…)
  • Make a pledge and set a delivery date
  • Track their pledge from pending to delivered
  • View their donation history and stats
NGO Admin
A staff member who manages their organization's needs.
  • Post donation needs with details and deadlines
  • Mark requests as urgent when critical
  • Review and confirm incoming pledges
  • Track progress toward each goal
  • View impact reports and summaries

What Data the System Stores
User Accounts profiles
Full name · the person's display name
Role · either "donor" or "ngo admin"
Phone · optional contact number
Linked to · their login account (email + password)
NGO Profiles ngos
Name · organization name
Location · where they operate
Contact email · public contact address
Verified · whether the NGO is approved
Donation Needs donation_needs
Item name · what is needed (e.g. "Rice bags")
Category · food / clothing / medicine / supplies
Quantity · how many are needed and pledged so far
Deadline · when the items are needed by
Urgency · normal or urgent
Pledges pledges
Quantity · how many units the donor is giving
Delivery date · when the donor will bring them
Notes · any special instructions
Status · pending, confirmed, or rejected
Status Labels Used in the App
Donation Need
Open — accepting donations Matched — goal reached Closed — no longer active
Pledge
Pending — waiting for NGO review Confirmed — NGO accepted it Rejected — NGO declined

Data Privacy & Security
Every user can only see data that belongs to them. Donors see only their own pledges. NGO admins see only their own organization's data. This is enforced at the database level — not just in the app — so there is no way for data to accidentally leak between users.
