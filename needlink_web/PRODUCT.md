# NeedLink — Product Context

## Product Purpose
NeedLink coordinates in-kind (physical item) donations between NGOs and individual donors in Uganda. No money changes hands — donors pledge physical items like food, clothing, medicine, or supplies to NGOs that post specific needs.

## Register
product

## Users

### Donor
Ordinary Ugandans who want to give back. They browse a live feed of NGO needs, pledge quantities of specific items, and track whether their pledges have been confirmed or delivered. Mobile-first. They care about trust (is this NGO real?), simplicity (can I pledge in under a minute?), and feedback (did my donation arrive?).

### NGO Admin
Staff at registered non-profits managing real logistics. They post specific needs with quantities and deadlines, review incoming pledges from donors, confirm or reject pledges, and confirm physical deliveries. They also view reports on match rates and delivery stats. Desktop-first for the dashboard.

## Brand

**Name:** NeedLink

**Tone:** Warm, trustworthy, action-oriented. Not charity-pitying. Treats donors as partners, not benefactors. Treats NGOs as professionals, not supplicants.

**Visual identity:**
- Primary: #0891B2 (compassion blue — trust, clarity, action)
- Accent: #EA580C (action orange — urgency, energy, calls to action)
- Background: #ECFEFF (light cyan — calm, clean, approachable)
- Foreground: #164E63 (deep teal — authority, depth)
- Typography: Fira Code (headings, data, monospaced labels) + Fira Sans (body, UI text)

**Anti-references:**
- Generic charity blue-and-white like GoFundMe
- Poverty-porn imagery or guilt-based copy
- Corporate SaaS purple gradients
- Cookie-cutter card grids with icon + heading + paragraph

## Strategic Principles

1. **Speed of trust.** A donor should be able to find a need, understand it, and pledge in under 60 seconds.
2. **Operational clarity.** NGO admins are managing real logistics. Every screen should reduce cognitive load, not add to it.
3. **Live over stale.** Realtime updates on needs and pledges are a core feature, not a bonus.
4. **Ugandan context.** Mobile data is limited. Pages should load fast and work on mid-range Android devices.

## Key Flows

1. Donor: Register → Browse needs → Pledge → Track status
2. NGO Admin: Register → Post need → Review pledges → Confirm delivery → View reports
